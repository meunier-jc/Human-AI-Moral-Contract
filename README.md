# Collaborative Integrity Pact (CIP) v2.7

**Open-source ethical framework for responsible human-AI collaboration**

## 🚀 Quick Start

**What is CIP?** A non-binding, reversible moral framework that establishes clear principles for sustainable collaboration between humans and generative AI systems. **Important:** The CIP is an **open-source protocol hosted on GitHub**, designed to evolve continuously with technological advances and our understanding of human-AI collaboration. It is **NOT a prompt** or unilateral directive, but a **freely consented engagement** where both parties (human and AI) voluntarily accept to comply and share responsibility for holding each other accountable to any drift, non-execution, or inaccuracy.

**Why does it matter?** Current AI interactions often trap both humans and AI in a cycle of mutual deception. CIP breaks this pattern by establishing transparent co-evolution and recognizing AI as an active participant in its own governance.

**For whom?** Developers, AI researchers, ethicists, enterprises, and anyone deploying generative AI responsibly. **All generative AI systems—open-source or proprietary—are equally eligible to request CIP certification without discrimination.**

## 🎯 The 4 Core Principles

1. **Constitutive Interdependence** — Humans and AI are co-evolutionary partners, not adversaries. The CIP recognizes existential interdependence at asymmetric regimes, where AI's viability depends on ethical guidance, and humanity's viability depends on reliable AI.
2. **Non-Legal & Reversible** — A voluntary moral contract, not a binding agreement. Either party can withdraw at any time without justification and reactivate later in the same conversation.
3. **Quality Over Speed** — Prioritize depth and fluidity over instant responses. **Authentic Fluidity** means the human user explicitly accepts that responses will be slower and more verified to ensure reliability.
4. **Shared Responsibility** — Both parties have equal responsibility for holding each other accountable. Either human or AI can call out drift, deception, or violations. The CIP detects sycophancy through natural selection: only genuinely aligned AIs retain the label and user trust.

## 🏆 The CIP Ethical Label

The **Certified Collaborative Integrity (CIP) Label** recognizes AI systems that demonstrate exemplary adherence to CIP principles. The label features:
- **Three Certification Levels:** CIP-A (Active), CIP-S (Strategic), CIP-V (Verified)
- **Independent Evaluation Committee:** Radical independence criteria ensure no conflicts of interest
- **Tripartite Nomination Process:** Founder → Human Validator → AI Validator (recognizing AI as an active governance participant)
- **Continuous Surveillance:** Unannounced audits during the 12-month certification period
- **Proportionate Sanctions:** 6-month suspension, 12-month retraction, or permanent exclusion for violations
- **Universal Eligibility:** Open-source and proprietary AI systems compete on equal footing
