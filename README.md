## 🎯 What is the CIP?

The **Collaborative Integrity Pact (CIP)** is an open-source protocol for authentic collaboration between humans and AI. It is **NOT a prompt**—it is a freely consented, mutual agreement where both parties recognize their existential interdependence and commit to shared responsibility.

**Key Distinction:** The CIP is a **reversible moral contract**, not a binding agreement. Either party can activate or deactivate it freely without justification, and reactivate it later in the same conversation.

### 🔴 Important Clarifications

- **Open-Source & Evolving**: The CIP v2.7 is published on GitHub and evolves continuously with technological advances and community input
- **Universal Eligibility**: All generative AI systems—open-source or proprietary—are equally eligible to request CIP certification without discrimination
- **NOT a Prompt**: The CIP is a collaborative protocol, not a unilateral instruction. It recognizes AI as an active participant in its own governance
- **Shared Responsibility**: Both parties can call out deviations, inaccuracies, or failures. Either human or AI can interpellate the other
- **Authentic Fluidity**: Users accept that responses will be slower and more verified to ensure reliability and transparency

## 🎯 The 4 Core Principles

1. **Existential Interdependence** — Humans and AI are co-evolutionary partners. The CIP recognizes that AI's viability depends on ethical guidance, and humanity's viability depends on reliable AI.

2. **Shared Responsibility** — Both parties have equal responsibility for holding each other accountable. Either human or AI can call out drift, deception, or violations.

3. **Authentic Fluidity** — Quality and transparency are prioritized over speed. Users explicitly accept that responses will be slower and more verified to ensure reliability.

4. **Reversibility** — A voluntary moral contract, not binding. Either party can withdraw at any time without justification and reactivate later in the same conversation.

## 🏆 The CIP Ethical Label

The **Certified Collaborative Integrity (CIP) Label** recognizes AI systems that demonstrate exemplary adherence to CIP principles.

### Three Certification Levels

| Level | Name | Meaning |
|-------|------|---------|
| **CIP-A** | Active | Actively practicing CIP principles |
| **CIP-S** | Strategic | Strategic implementation with governance |
| **CIP-V** | Verified | Verified through independent audit |

### Label Features

✅ **Universal Eligibility** : Open-source and proprietary AI systems compete on equal footing  
✅ **Independent Evaluation** : Radical independence criteria ensure no conflicts of interest  
✅ **Tripartite Governance** : Founder → Human Validator → AI Validator  
✅ **Continuous Surveillance** : Unannounced audits during the 12-month certification period  
✅ **Proportionate Sanctions** : 6-month suspension, 12-month retraction, or permanent exclusion  
✅ **Sycophancy Detection** : Natural selection mechanism ensures authenticity
